## Firefox’s Story

Firefox is a values-driven company, inviting people into a two-way conversation and connecting a user's choice to the bigger picture of a more open, private, and accessible internet. Our mission is building an internet that puts people first, empowering them to shape their own experience while feeling safe and independent. The web was meant to expand our user’s world, with them at the helm. But somewhere along the way, they became just a passenger. Defaults set the standard. One-size-fits-all algorithms shaped our experiences. Profits were prioritized and manipulation became the norm. For most of us, the internet isn’t just a tool. It’s where we live, learn, create, connect, wander and escape. It’s imperfect. It’s personal. It’s always changing. And it should be yours to shape. We remember what the internet was meant to be, and we know it can be that again. It’s time for an internet that’s honest and transparent, one where people are the priority. We’re not here to trap our user’s attention — we’re here to give it back. We believe in a web where privacy is the default, not the upgrade. Where discovery isn’t driven by surveillance. Where control belongs to them. Do emphasize:

- emphasize autonomy

- privacy-by-default

- user control

- transparency

Don’t use:

- fearmongering

- exaggeration

- absolute claims

- surveillance implications
